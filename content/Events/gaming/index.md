# Gaming at Anthrocon

{{< center >}}
Video Gaming at Anthrocon is located on the Second Floor of the Westin Hotel. We have a wide array of console games for play, as well as Indie game displays, and this year we have added Arcade Games to the lineup!

[Tabletop Gaming](#tabletop-gaming) - [Video Gaming](#video-gaming) - [Mega Cat Studios](#mega-cat-studios-and-indie-premier)

## Tabletop Gaming

{{< columns >}}

Tabletop gaming is located on the second floor of the Westin Hotel. We offer many gaming events throughout the weekend in addition to open-play spaces for board games, RPGs, and card games!

From classic titles to the wackiest party games, casuals and hardcore gamers alike can sit back and relax (or get their sweat on) in our gaming room. There are a variety of games that our team curates and brings each year, with some tournaments and events that happen each convention day!

Anthrocon streams many gaming events throughout the weekend for both video games and our Charity Poker tournament. Check the convention schedule for the full list and times of events.

## Mega Cat Studios and "Indie Premier"

{{< columns >}}

The Indie Premier is a showcase built for the future of indie gaming, highlighting titles on the radar for game developers and enthusiasts. Partnering with Unity®PGH and Mega Cat Studios, the team scans the global landscape to find the signal in the noise, spotlighting unconventional genre blends, striking visual directions, and deeply passionate stories from next-generation creators. Taking place in both digital and physical formats, this hybrid event maximizes global reach and onsite interaction. Selected participants will have the opportunity to showcase their games in person and online, competing for physical trophies and digital award laurels.

Press play on the future of indie. Submit your GAME now!
