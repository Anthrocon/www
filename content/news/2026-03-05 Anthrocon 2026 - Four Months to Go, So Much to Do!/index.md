It’s hard to believe we’re only four months now and SO much has already happened \- for better or worse\! But the hardworking team at Anthrocon never stops, much like the creatures of mystery and legend that dwell in Pittsburgh.

## Registration

Online pre-registration will be **paused temporarily between March 8 and 10** to help support our General hotel block opening on March 10 (more information in the next section). After the opening, pre-registration will resume until March 10, 2026 **OR** if we reach our designated maximum capacity. For more information and to register, check out our [**Registration page’s “How to Register” section**](https://www.anthrocon.org/registration/#how-to-register).

## Hotel Accommodations

As with last month’s announcement, we had postponed the General hotel block opening to March 10 to allow for the hotel systems team to apply fixes to the system. The hotel booking link will be sent at 12 PM Eastern Time on March 10 to pre-registered Attending and Sponsor level groups \- you will not receive a hotel link before then. More information about the process, hotel options and additional tips can be found on our [**Hotels page**](https://www.anthrocon.org/hotel/).

No matter where you might end up staying during the con, we’re working to improve our courtesy transportation service for Anthrocon attendees. While we work on updating shuttle information for 2026, you can reference last year’s service as an example on our **[Shuttle Buses page](https://www.anthrocon.org/shuttle-buses/)**.

## Artists Alley

Our Artists Alley rules and process for 2026 is available on our site\! The Artists Alley is a great way for budding artists to showcase their talents and artistic wares. The sign ups for the Artists Alley are a lottery, meaning that results are randomized and will be different for each day it’s open. Learn about the latest updates for 2026 and how to apply on our [**Artists Alley page**](https://www.anthrocon.org/alley/)\!

## Dance Competition Events, Panel and Performance Applications

Two of our biggest dance competition events, the Fursuit Dance Competition and Floor Wars, have now been updated on our website with 2026 information\! If you’re an interested competitor waiting for their moment to shine on the brightest (and spookiest) stage in the fandom, don’t miss these opportunities. Read more about the rules, process and applications on our [**Fursuit Dance Competition page**](https://www.anthrocon.org/dance-competition/) and our [**Floor Wars page**](https://www.anthrocon.org/floor-wars/)\!

Applications for performing and hosting events are closing soon, if they haven’t already\! Don’t wait any longer if you’re thinking of contributing your knowledge and/or skills to our community. Form links can be found on our **[Events page](https://www.anthrocon.org/events-panels/)** and our [**Stage Performances page**](https://www.anthrocon.org/stage-performances/).

For those looking to apply to Anthrocon Tonight, our signature stage show in a more intimate environment, a separate form will be announced in the coming months.

## Advertising and Conbook Submissions

There are fewer better ways to market yourself or your business than at Anthrocon, where we have a literal city of furries reading and looking at all our material. If you’re interested in having your creative works or advertisements featured in our conbook or as paid advertising, our forms are now open online\!

Head over to our [**Advertising page**](https://www.anthrocon.org/advertising/) or our **[Conbook Submissions page](https://www.anthrocon.org/conbook-submissions/)** for more details, guidelines and forms.

## River Cruises

River Cruises tickets are still available for 2026\! Whether you’re looking for awesome photo opportunities or want to enjoy beautiful sunset on the river, these cruises are a hot attraction for Anthrocon attendees. You can find all the update pricing for tickets that set sail from Thursday to Sunday of the convention by going onto our Registration system and viewing the “Event Store”. For other details, visit the [**River Cruises page**](https://update-reg-details-2026.anthrocon.pages.dev/anthrocon-river-cruises/).

## Staff and volunteer opportunities

Even in a dark and spooky forest, a convention like Anthrocon shines the brightest when people step up and help it run smoothly. Every aspect of our event is run by volunteers of different levels; whether you’ve got the most. If you’re willing to contribute your time before or at the convention, let us know by completing the Volunteering section in our [**Registration system**](https://reg.anthrocon.org/apply/volunteer) (you must create an account first)\!

## Stay up to date with latest announcements

While many of us are getting ready to spend some much-deserved holiday time being warm and cozy (and especially away from the dark, murky woods\!), we want to make sure you come into the new year with the latest information as soon as it’s available. Make sure to follow us on our official channels on BlueSky, Facebook, X (formerly Twitter), and our community channels such as the official Discord server for Anthrocon. You can find links to all those platforms on the bottom of our website pages\!
